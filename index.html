HOW TO USE

Adjust the donut chart to find what you'd like using the controls. Click on a slice of the donut chart to view information about the cryptocurrency. Once, the information is displayed you can click on "Change Chart" to switch to a candlestick graph displaying historical price over the past year (in some cases you have to click the pie slice twice).

(Due to the API used, some currencies may not have historical data)

GLOSSARY

Symbol:

This is the shorthand representation of the currency which is also found when trading (buying/selling).

Price:

The current valuation (purchase/sell value) for the currency.

Market Cap:

The market capitalization is the market value of total currency. It is calculated by multiplying the price with the total outstanding shares.

Percent Change:

This is the percent change in price over a specific time frame. (Here it is represented as either 24 hours or 7 days)


**The ranking is determined by market cap**
