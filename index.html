HOW TO USE

Adjust the donut chart to find what you'd like using the controls. Click on a slice of the donut chart to view information about the cryptocurrency. Once, the information is displayed you can click on "Change Chart" to switch to a candlestick graph displaying historical price over the past year (in some cases you have to click the pie slice twice).

(Due to the API used, some currencies may not have historical data)
